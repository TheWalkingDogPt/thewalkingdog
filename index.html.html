Where we walk

Cascais is
our playground.

No extra charge for any location. We cover the whole area.

01
Praia do Guincho
Wild Atlantic beach with dramatic dunes. Our most iconic location — nothing else in the area looks like this.
02
Cascais Marina
Yachts, cobblestone, and the sea. A beautiful waterfront walk your dog will love.
03
Boca do Inferno
Dramatic cliffs and crashing waves. One of Cascais's most iconic spots, unmistakable in every photo.
04
Parque Marechal Carmona
Cascais's central park with peacocks, fountains and shaded paths. Perfect for calmer walks.
05
Sintra-Cascais Park
Lush forest trails connecting Cascais to Sintra. Our adventure walk — great for active breeds.
06
Estoril Promenade
The palm-lined seafront between Estoril and Cascais. Flat, scenic, and perfect for morning walks.
